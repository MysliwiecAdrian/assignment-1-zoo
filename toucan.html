Toucan Exhibit

Welcome to the Toucan Exhibit!

Toucans are tropical birds known for their oversized, colorful bills, which can be as long as their body. Native to Central and South American rainforests, their bills help them reach fruit on distant branches and regulate body temperature. Despite their large beaks, toucans are light and agile flyers. Their loud calls echo through the forest, and they often live in small flocks, adding a splash of color to the treetops with their vibrant feathers.
