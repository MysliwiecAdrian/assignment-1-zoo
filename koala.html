Koala Exhibit

Welcome to the Koala Exhibit!

Koalas are tree-dwelling marsupials from Australia, known for their fluffy ears and relaxed lifestyle. They sleep up to 20 hours a day due to their low-energy diet of eucalyptus leaves. Despite their sleepy nature, koalas are skilled climbers, using sharp claws to grip trees. Unfortunately, habitat loss and disease threaten their survival, making conservation vital.
