Welcome to the Giraffe Exhibit!

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, with necks that can reach up to 6 feet long, allowing them to eat leaves from the tops of trees. They have unique spotted coats, and no two giraffes have the same pattern. Giraffes' long tongues, which can be up to 20 inches, help them grasp food and avoid thorns.
